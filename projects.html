Bookstagram

The Bookstagram website is a project meant to resemble a modern social media site, while also providing a space where readers can go to see what the top trending books are. I’d wanted to use an API to grab the real-time most popular books and display them as fake “posts” that you could like and share.

Comic-Con

The Comic-Con Design is meant to be a marketing project for the famous comic convention. It’s a book for the attendees to read while walking around the event. I’d wanted the design to have a comic book feel. I included a table of contents, as well as keynote speakers and other important information for the event.
